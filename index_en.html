About

Felipe Mattar, creator of ROCPAIN GAMES, holds a Master of Arts from the Postgraduate Program in Arts at the Federal University of Espírito Santo, where he developed research on the use of sound hyperrealism in Brazilian contemporary fiction cinema, and a Bachelor's Degree in Industrial Design (Graphic Design/UFES). Substitute professor in the Design department/UFES between 2018 and 2019. Founding partner of EXP Movies and Artistic Productions which, between 2009 and 2019, developed projects in the cultural, cinema and audiovisual areas. He has experience in several audiovisual fields with a focus on editing, design and sound mixing. In 2016, he received the award for best sound editing at the 20th Cine PE (Recife / PE), for the medium-length film "Das Águas que Passam" by Diego Zon. He is currently also dedicated to the development of electronic games, since the launch of the ROCPAIN game in 2018.
